About ARC#6014

More Than Robots

Inspired by the motto of FIRST, ARC is deeply involved in projects to spread STEM education across Turkey and beyond. We believe in building not just robots, but the future.

Our Mission

To inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.

Passion

We truly enjoy building robots and pushing the boundaries of innovation.

Excellence

Striving for perfection in every competition and project we undertake.

Teamwork

Collaboration and mutual support drive our success and growth.

Innovation

Creating solutions that inspire and transform the future of robotics.

Meet Our Team

Our strength comes from the dedication of every subgroup and its members. Each person plays a vital role in bringing our vision to life.

As a school team, Robert College students from 9th to 12th grades take part in our robotics journey. From brainstorming to building, preparing projects to fundraising, many aspects of our involvement is mainly student-led.
